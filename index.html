about this project

What:

This website is being created as part of an independent study course in Aviation History.

When:

This project will be completed over the course of the 2020-2021 school year, but I may decide to continue adding to it thereafter.

Where:

South Brunswick High School; Monmouth Junction, NJ

Who:

My name is John Yang (Class of 2022), and I'm working with Mr. Christopher Hines who will be supervising me this year. Mr. Hines is a Social Studies teacher here at SBHS.

Why:

My goals in creating this website are as follows:

  • To create a repository in which I can creatively demonstrate the knowledge I will gain during this course
  • To express my love and passion for aviation
  • To inspire others to explore flight
