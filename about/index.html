A bit about me:

Hi! I'm John, a junior at South Brunswick High School in Monmouth Junction, NJ. Besides aviation, I enjoy cooking and learning about pretty much anything, but especially about science.

Huge thanks to Mr. Christopher Hines for working with me on this independent study course.

My goals

  • To create a space where I can demonstrate the knowledge I will gain during this course
